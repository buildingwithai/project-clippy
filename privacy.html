Privacy Policy for Project Clippy

Effective Date: April 7, 2025

Project Clippy is a Chrome extension that helps users save and reuse text snippets for faster workflows. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about what data we collect and how it is used.

What Data We Collect

1. Locally Stored Snippets

  • Text snippets you choose to save using the extension
  • Optional titles you assign to your snippets

These are stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. We do not transmit or sync this data to external servers.

2. Optional User Accounts (Pro Version)

  • We may collect your email address for account creation and communication
  • We may offer sync features that store your data securely on our servers

3. Payment Information

If you upgrade to a paid plan, your payment will be securely processed via a third-party provider (e.g., Stripe or Gumroad). We do not collect or store your credit card or billing details directly.

How We Use Your Data

  • To save and organize your snippets for personal use
  • To provide Pro features (like syncing or account login, if enabled)
  • To communicate with you regarding account-related information (Pro users only)
  • To improve the extension’s functionality and user experience (e.g. usage analytics if you opt in)

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not track your web activity outside of the snippet-saving interaction
  • We do not access or store any personal information unless you provide it explicitly

Data Security

  • Snippets are stored using Chrome’s secure local storage API
  • Any synced data (Pro version only) is encrypted and securely stored
  • We follow industry best practices to protect your data

Your Choices

  • You can delete your saved snippets anytime
  • You can uninstall the extension to stop all data collection
  • If you create an account (Pro), you can request account deletion at any time
